1) New User Registration
    

When you open a Attendance system first time, then you must registered your self. Registration procedure are below
   Step 1: Click on new user?
   Step 2: Select Your Name (Note: If your name is not present in List then please contact Admin of system)
   Step 3: Enter New password and Confirm password (Note: Password must be greater than five character)
   Step 4: Select Security Question
   Step 5: Enter Answer for your security question.
   Step 6: Submit
   Now you can Login into system...
